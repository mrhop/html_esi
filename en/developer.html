The Blue Blood

ESI Group is owned and running by Tucher family. Tucher family is one of the oldest and richest family in Germany. Since 700 years ago, Tucher family started the business with nothing, till now they have already become the most effective family, from local company to a global one.

Since 1503, Tucher Funding was built. Till now Tucher family has accumulated the experience in constructions for more than 500 years. We brought our rich experience and technics into China through the project of Noble Mansion.

Tucher family has a deep fate with China. In middle century, Tucher family brought cloth and spice trade into China via the Silk Road. Now, we brought all kinds of projects to China those related to constructions, environment and energy. Besides, Noble Mansion will be the new symbol and witness for the friendship between China and Germany.
